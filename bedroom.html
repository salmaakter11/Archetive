Working Process

Our Process for Design Your Dream

01
Initial Consultation and Brief Development

Begins with a meeting to understand the client's vision and requirements, followed by the creation of a detailed project brief outlining the scope, budget, and design preferences.

02
Design Concept and Presentation

The designer develops a creative design concept, including mood boards and layouts, which is then presented to the client for feedback and revisions.

03
Technical Documentation and Procurement

Involves creating detailed drawings and specifications for construction, as well as sourcing and ordering the necessary materials, furniture, and fittings.

04
Implementation and Project Management

The design is brought to life through construction and installation, overseen by the designer to ensure fidelity to the design plan, culminating in styling, final walkthrough, and project handover .
